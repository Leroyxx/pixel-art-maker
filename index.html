Instructions

  1. Supply the width, height and size of every square side for your grid and hit "Make Grid!" to create your dream canvas of squares.
  2. Choose a color to draw with from the palette (left click) or pick a custom color. Right click a square on the palette to change eraser's color.
  3. Click (and continue to drag if you wish) the mouse over the squares in the canvas in order to draw.
  4. Erase by right clicking (and continuing to drag, again, if you wish) with your mouse.
  5. Hold CTRL and right or left click a square on the palette to store your eraser's or pen's color in it correspondingly.
  6. Your canvas of squares will be minimized and displayed on the bottom left.
  7. If you wish to undo, press CTRL and Z. Adding SHIFT to the combo will redo.
  8. Be careful not to overwrite over wanted redo's after undoing, going back to the future has yet to have been coded.

PixelTip: You can save your pixel creation in PixelCode and then load it in the future that is near and far.
